NK NEWSLETTER MVP CONTEXT
Version: 2026-05-08
Use: Put this file in the repo root as NK_NEWSLETTER_MVP_CONTEXT.txt before running Codex Prompt 1.

PROJECT GOAL
Build a free, standalone internal newsletter MVP for NK.

The newsletter should produce a weekday internal market brief focused only on:
- AI + fashion
- AI + beauty
- AI + e-commerce
- agentic commerce
- AI shopping
- AI agents or assistants used by major fashion, beauty, retail, and commerce platforms or brands

This is not a generic fashion newsletter.
This is not a generic e-commerce newsletter.
This is not a generic AI newsletter.

CALL-DERIVED REQUIREMENT
Cyril described:
- a simple daily RSS/news scrape;
- around 8 a.m.;
- likely weekdays, not weekends;
- small internal recipient list, roughly five people;
- simple HTML newsletter;
- not too many images;
- main big titles;
- one large lead story and then a simple grid/two-column layout;
- example source categories: Vogue Business, Business of Fashion, AI + fashion, AI + beauty, AI + e-commerce, agentic commerce;
- example alert type: if Nike has a new agent, the team should know.

FREE MVP ARCHITECTURE
Use the simplest free architecture:
- Standalone Node 20 ESM CLI.
- Google News RSS query feeds as primary ingestion.
- rss-parser as the only dependency.
- Built-in fetch.
- Built-in node:test.
- Deterministic filtering and summaries.
- No LLM.
- No database.
- No paid APIs.
- No page scraping in v1.
- Local HTML/text/run.json output.
- Static site output in site/.
- GitHub Actions weekday scheduler.
- GitHub Actions artifact upload by default.
- Optional GitHub Pages static preview.
- Optional Resend free-tier email only after preview approval.

DEFAULT DISTRIBUTION
Default distribution is free and credential-free:
- Generate .newsletter-outbox/YYYY-MM-DD/newsletter.html
- Generate .newsletter-outbox/YYYY-MM-DD/newsletter.txt
- Generate .newsletter-outbox/YYYY-MM-DD/run.json
- Generate site/index.html
- Generate site/newsletter.txt
- Generate site/run.json
- Upload .newsletter-outbox and site as GitHub Actions artifacts.

OPTIONAL EMAIL
Email must be optional and disabled by default.
Never send unless:
- NEWSLETTER_SEND_ENABLED=true
- RESEND_API_KEY exists
- NEWSLETTER_FROM exists
- NEWSLETTER_TO has at least one valid email
- mode is send or auto inside the target time window
- item count >= NEWSLETTER_MIN_ITEMS

Do not hard-code recipient emails.
Do not hard-code API keys.
Do not log secrets.
Do not log full recipient lists.

INCLUSION RULES
Include an item if:
- it contains a high-priority phrase, OR
- it contains at least one AI/agentic term and at least one fashion/beauty/retail/shopping/ecommerce/commerce/brand term.

High-priority phrases:
- agentic commerce
- AI shopping
- AI stylist
- AI search
- AI product discovery
- AI recommendations
- AI retail
- AI beauty
- AI fashion
- virtual try-on
- instant checkout
- commerce protocol
- product feed
- catalog data
- Shopify Catalog
- Agentic Storefronts

EXCLUSION RULES
Exclude:
- generic fashion news without AI
- generic beauty news without AI
- generic e-commerce news without AI
- generic AI news without fashion, beauty, retail, shopping, commerce, or brand relevance
- celebrity or runway-only stories
- tariffs, earnings, operations, or supply-chain stories unless AI is central

OUTPUT STYLE
HTML newsletter:
- white background
- black text
- system font
- no external CSS
- no external images in v1
- one lead story
- remaining stories in responsive two-column cards
- source name, category, date, and Read source link
- footer: Internal NK market brief. Summaries are deterministic and based on RSS metadata only.

SAFETY
- Internal-only.
- No customer emails.
- No Shopify customer data.
- No public signup flow.
- No full article scraping.
- No raw feed HTML rendering.
- No invented article facts.
- No invented NK, Norma, product, policy, archive, or customer claims.
- No UGC, avatar, or virtual try-on implementation.
- Real email disabled by default.
